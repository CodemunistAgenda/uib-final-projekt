Colorless

Colorless

Colorless entities in Magic: The Gathering, such as Eldrazi and artifacts, are outside of the traditional color wheel. These beings are often ancient, powerful, and neutral in their affiliations. Artifacts represent technology and magical objects, while Eldrazi are mysterious and consume entire planes.

Standard Modern Commander Pioneer Legacy

Standard

Standard is a dynamic format where players build decks using cards from the most recent Magic: The Gathering sets.

Modern

Modern is a format where players can use cards from core sets and expansions going back to 8th Edition.

Commander

Commander is a multiplayer-focused format where players build decks around a legendary creature, known as the Commander.

Pioneer

Pioneer is a relatively new format that allows cards from sets as far back as Return to Ravnica.

Legacy

Legacy is an eternal format where cards from almost all Magic sets are legal, but it has a restricted ban list.
